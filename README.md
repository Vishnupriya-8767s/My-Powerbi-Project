**Global Store Sales Analytics Dashboard**

  An interactive business intelligence dashboard project for analyzing Global Store sales and profitability across multiple dimensions (time, geography, product, and customer segments).
  
**Project overview**

  This project delivers a two page analytics dashboard that helps stakeholders quickly understand overall performance and then drill down into detailed insights.
  The first page focuses on high level KPIs and dimensional breakdowns, while the second page emphasizes time series and financial year trends.
  Features

**Page 1 – Executive overview**
 
  •	Key metrics tiles: Total Profit, Total Sales, Total Quantity, Order Count, Sum of No Profit, and Profit Margin %.
  
  •	Discount by Category: Donut/pie chart showing profit contribution by Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology.
  
  •	Shipping Cost by Ship Mode: Donut chart summarizing total shipping cost split by First Class, Second Class, Standard Class, and Same Day.
  
  •	Quantity by Segment: Donut chart showing order quantity by Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office.
  
  •	Sales by Country: World map visualizing sales and profit distribution across regions and countries.
  
  •	Top 5 Sub Category by Profit: Bar chart displaying the most profitable sub categories (e.g., Bookcases, Chairs, Copiers, Phones, Storage).
  
  •	Below 5 Sub Category by Profit: Column/waterfall style chart highlighting the least profitable sub categories.
  
  •	Interactive slicers: Order Date range, Category, Region, and Segment filters that update all visuals in real time.

**Page 2 – Time series analysis**

  •	KPI tiles: Start of Year value, End of Year value, First Class shipping cost, and Average delivery days.
  
  •	Calendar matrix: Year–Month table with Sum of Sales, Total MTD (Month to Date), Total QTD (Quarter to Date), and Total YTD (Year to Date).
  
  •	Profit by Year and Category: Area/line chart showing profit trends over time for Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology.

  •	Profit Financial Year YTD: Column chart tracking profit across months and quarters for each financial year.
